Network Rules

Play fair, respect others, and enjoy the grind. Breaking rules leads to mutes, kicks, or bans.

Cheating & Clients

  • No hacked clients, macros, or autoclickers (including the F3+T inbuilt keybind trick).
  • No x-ray, ESP, or unfair texture packs.
  • Staff may request a Screenshare (SS) or log check.

Exploits & Bugs

  • No abusing glitches, dupes, or loopholes.
  • No bypassing combat systems or anti-cheats.
  • Report bugs to staff for potential rewards.

Fair Gameplay

  • No boosting, win-trading, or stat manipulation.
  • No cross-teaming in solo game modes.
  • No farming kills using alternate accounts.

Chat Behavior

  • No hate speech, harassment, or doxxing.
  • No excessive spamming or advertising.
  • No impersonating staff or other players.

Account Safety

  • You are responsible for all actions on your account.
  • Ban evasion on alts results in a permanent IP ban.
  • Keep your account secure; do not share passwords.

Community & Store

  • No chargebacks or payment fraud in the store.
  • No scams, malicious links, or fake giveaways.
  • Follow staff instructions in support tickets.

Store & Payments

  • No fraudulent payments or chargebacks. Doing so results in a network-wide ban.
  • All purchases are final. Refunds are only handled via official support tickets.
  • Virtual items may take up to 15 minutes to arrive in-game.

Important Note

  • Rules are subject to change at any time to ensure fair play.
  • Not knowing a rule is not an excuse for breaking it.
  • Staff have the final say in all punishment disputes.
