Privacy & Policy

Official AstralyxPvP Terms & Conditions document.

AstralyxPvP values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: username, email address, IP address, and other information you provide when creating or using an account.
  • Gameplay Data: in-game actions, server activity, statistics, progress, and virtual items.
  • Support Data: messages, reports, or other information you send to staff or through our support systems.
  • Website Data: cookies, browser type, device information, and similar technical data if you use our website.

Plain language: We collect information about your account, gameplay, website use, and anything you send to our staff.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • provide, operate, and improve AstralyxPvP;
  • maintain account security and prevent cheating, abuse, fraud, or rule-breaking;
  • respond to support requests and communicate with you about your account or our services;
  • analyze gameplay trends and server performance;
  • enforce our Terms of Service and server rules.

Plain language: We use your info to keep the server running, safe, and fun.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information:

  • with staff members for moderation, support, and security purposes;
  • with trusted service providers that help us run our network, such as hosting, website tools, email services, analytics, or support tools;
  • if required by law, court order, or government request;
  • if necessary to protect AstralyxPvP, our users, or the public.

Plain language: Your information is not for sale. We only share it when needed to run the network or follow the law.

4. Cookies and Tracking

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not work correctly if you do.

Plain language: Cookies help the site work better, and you can turn them off in your browser.

5. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including account management, security, moderation, legal compliance, and dispute handling.

Inactive accounts may be deleted or anonymized after a reasonable period, unless we need to keep certain data for legal, security, or operational reasons.

Plain language: We keep data while we need it, and old inactive data may be removed later.

Depending on your location and applicable law, you have the right to:

To make a request, contact us using the email below.

Plain language: You can ask to see, fix, or delete your data in some situations.
